Only available in one frame size but the telescoping stem and seat post offer some fit adjustment, two color choices (black or white), optional steel fenders and alloy racks (front and rear) but no light options. Slightly upgraded Shimano Altus derailleur, good kickstand choice and positioning, plastic chainring guard prevents chain drops and an alloy support bar keeps the bike stable when folded but no magnets or bands to keep it folded. Folding fat tire bikes balance approachability with comfort, increased air volume reduces the need for suspension, tire pressure can be lowered for use on trails, packed snow, and even sand, the bike weighs more than average at 60lbs, the rims aren't punched out, no USB charging ports, key must be left in to ride.
