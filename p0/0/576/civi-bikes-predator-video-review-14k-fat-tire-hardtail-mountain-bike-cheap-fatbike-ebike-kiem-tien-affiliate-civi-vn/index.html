Description

https://electricbikereview.com/civi-bikes/predator/ The CIVI BIKES Predator is a powerful fat tire electric trail bike with adjustable spring suspension fork offering 120mm travel, rack and fender bosses, upgraded Selle Royal saddle, padded grips, and rubberized brake levers add comfort. High capacity battery pack extends the range of this ebike and allows for more frequent twist throttle use, it's mounted low and center for balance and is partially sunk into the frame for aesthetics and protection. Compact control pad is reachable and stays out of the way, built-in USB port on battery allows for accessory charging on the go, slightly upgraded derailleur, good kickstand choice and positioning, plastic chainring guard prevents chain drops. Heavier than some competing models at ~62lbs because the rims are not punched out and it uses a spring fork vs. air, only available in high-step frame style and one default size, impressively low price point, direct order only but available in multiple countries and they are working on adding dealers.
